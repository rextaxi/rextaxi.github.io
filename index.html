Welcome to Rex Taxi Services

We offer reliable, comfortable, and convenient transportation solutions in Blantyre, Malawi. Our well-maintained fleet and professional drivers ensure you have a pleasant journey every time.

About Us

Rex Taxi Services, established in 2010 by today's owner, a former Avis employe for 5 years, started with just one taxi and has grown into a trusted transportation provider with five well-maintained, air-conditioned cars. Our drivers are fluent in English, well-trained in safe driving practices, and possess excellent knowledge of the city's and country's geography.

Our Services

  • Self-Drive: Experience the freedom of driving yourself with our self-drive service. Enjoy the comfort and convenience of our well-maintained vehicles.
  • Chauffeur Drive: Sit back and relax while our professional drivers take you to your destination. Our friendly and fluent English-speaking drivers are here to ensure your comfort and safety.
  • Airport Shuttle: Our airport shuttle service guarantees timely and stress-free transfers to and from the airport.
  • City Tours: Explore the beauty of Blantyre with our city tour service. Our knowledgeable drivers will guide you through the city's attractions.
  • Hotel Transfers: We provide seamless hotel transfers, ensuring you reach your accommodation comfortably.
  • National Parks and Game Reserves: Plan an unforgettable trip to Malawi's national parks and game reserves with our expert drivers. Discover the stunning wildlife and natural beauty of the region.
  • Lake Malawi Trips: Enjoy a relaxing trip to Lake Malawi with our comfortable and reliable transportation.
  • Cross-Border Drives: We offer cross-border drives for your convenience. Travel beyond Malawi with ease and comfort.
